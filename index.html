Game Reviews

Sea Eagles 24 Cowboys 12

The Sea Eagles played well with Curtis Sironen and Danny Levi pushing over to score in the first half and Daly Cherry-Evans. and Cade Cust going over in the second half. The Cowboys played gritty but Kyle Feldt and Francis Molo went over. The Cowboys missed some trys like Coen Hess knocking the ball on over the line and the Hammer's 70 metre kick return.

Storm 46 Broncos 8

The Storm played excellent with Josh Addo-Carr, Tino Faasuamaleaui, and Jahrome Hughes all getting doubles. The Broncos are in trouble with back-to-back loses against the Tigers 48-0 and now the Storm 46-8.
